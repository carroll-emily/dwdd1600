About Me

An Artist from the Beginning

Emily Carroll is a Utah-based graphic designer who currently resides in Provo, studying graphic design at Utah Valley University as a full-time student.

She was born in August 2003 in Logan, UT as the youngest of 4 with her twin brother, Alex. She was raised in Layton, UT and graduated highschool with Honors in 2022 with a focus in humanitarian efforts, art and literature and scientific studies. Her parents commented on how she was always seen drawing, painting or creating as a child, but she didn't get serious about art until the middle of her high school education.

Since graduating, Emily has been studying at UVU, but took a break to serve an LDS mission in Bakersfield California, speaking English and American Sign Language. She has recently returned and is excited to focus on her education.

Some noteable awards she has recieved include: Percussionist of the Year-LHS 2022, Young Women Medallion-2019, Judges Recognition-UVU institute art gallery 2023, and 2nd place-reflections contest, photography.

In her free time, Emily enjoys playing video games, binging popular movies or tv shows, or appreciating art with her friends at local museums or galleries.
