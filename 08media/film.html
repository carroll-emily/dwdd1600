My Years in Percussion

During my Jr. High and High School Education, I was blessed with many opportunities to grow as an artist outside of the visual realm. I participated in Percussive arts, theatre and choir for 6 years. Over the course of those years, I gained recognition as a leader in the drumline and band as I explored my love of these are forms.
